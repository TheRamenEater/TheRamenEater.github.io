The Origin Of Ramen is unclear. Like most Japanese food it originated from China.

It was called shina soba which means Chinese Soba (Soba is a type of noodle FYI). They started to call it Ramen in thes 1950s. Eventually Ramen became so popular that it was accompanied by a new type of Ramen, instant Ramen (Known as cup Ramen in Japan FYI).
